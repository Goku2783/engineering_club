II. Steps of the Engineering Design Process (EDP)

  1. Ask - Define the problem?
  2. Imagine - Think of solutions.
  3. Plan - Make a plan. (Think! Sketch! Label!)
  4. Create - Make a prototype and test it.
  5. Improve - How can you modify your design to make it better?
